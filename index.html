TANGENT

Reading groups for students who want to think like researchers.

Philosophy

Tangent runs small reading groups. The goal is not to finish a syllabus — it is to learn how to read hard material carefully, ask sharp questions, and develop taste for what matters.

Most students are never shown how researchers actually read. They skim, they Google, they use AI as an answer machine. Tangent is an attempt to slow that down: to sit with a paper or a chapter long enough to disagree with it, reconstruct its argument, and push past the point where it gets difficult.

Two groups, two levels. Both meet weekly. Both are small by design.
