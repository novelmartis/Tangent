What this is

Not tutoring. Not passive class. A mentored reading group.

Students read serious material each week, then discuss it in small groups with a mentor. Good questions matter more than polished answers.

Why this format

Read slowly. Think clearly.

Each week has one core text. Students read before session, then discuss what it says, why it matters, and what is still unclear.

One text each week

A manageable reading load, done with care.

Small-group discussion

Enough room for each student to think out loud.

Growing responsibility

Mentors lead first, then students lead more each week.

How this started

It began as informal reading sessions.

Tangent grew out of informal reading sessions in India. Several students later entered IIT programs, and another continued into postgraduate physics study in Europe. Later, the same format also worked with undergraduate and Master’s students in Osnabrück.
