What Tangent is.

Small reading groups

Each group is intentionally small so students can be known well and discussion can stay thoughtful.

Grades 7–12

Tangent is for students who are ready for deeper reading, better questions, and slower, more careful understanding.

Subjects

The three current directions are Physics, Mathematics, and Intelligent Behavior, with more coming later.

Format

Online, mentor-led, over four weeks, with core reading and one live session each week.
