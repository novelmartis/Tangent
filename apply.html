What happens next

We read every application. If it seems like a good fit, we schedule a short call to talk through placement, timing, and questions.

Pricing

Tangent is paid. The standard fee for the Foundational Track is ₹4,000 per student for the four-week group in India, with subsidies discussed where needed.

For students outside India, fees are adjusted for purchasing-power differences.
