Fees

We try to keep Tangent accessible.

The standard India fee for a four-week Tangent group is ₹4,000 per student. We have tried to keep this affordable for most families while still respecting the time of expert mentors.

Mentors are offering their time at far below normal professional rates because they see Tangent as an educational and social project.

If the fee is a genuine barrier, subsidies can be discussed case by case. For families outside India, fees may be adjusted using purchasing-power parity.
