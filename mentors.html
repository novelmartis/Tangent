Mentors who enjoy helping students think.

Tangent mentors are researchers, professionals, and deep readers.

What makes a Tangent mentor different?
1

Chooses serious but approachable texts.

The reading should stretch students without becoming a performance.

2

Helps confusion become productive.

Confusion is treated as a place to begin, not something to hide.

3

Asks better questions.

Mentors avoid quick answers when a sharper question would do more.

4

Models intellectual honesty.

Students see what careful thinking sounds like in real time.
