On mentoring

The role is not to rush students through material.

Tangent mentors are not there to over-explain everything or optimize students for performance. They help students stay with hard material, ask better questions, and gradually become less dependent on guidance.

In spirit, this owes more to apprenticeship and serious mentorship than to conventional tutoring.
