Tracks

Choose the track that matches where you are right now.

Both tracks run for four weeks with a mentor. The main difference is what you read and how technical discussion gets.

Track overview

Foundational and Advanced

Foundational

Middle school / early secondary Roughly Grades 7–9 2–3 students 4 weeks

For younger students ready to move beyond school-style learning. Reading centers on textbook chapters and clear foundational material.

  • Good for serious younger students who want depth without performance pressure.
  • Themes often include Physics, Mathematics, and AI/Cognitive Neuroscience.
  • Current offerings: Feynman Lectures, Proofs from the Book, and Braitenberg's Vehicles.
Go to Foundational Track →

Advanced

Advanced high school / undergraduate 4–6 students 4 weeks

For older students ready for serious paper-based discussion. Reading centers on one research paper each week, sometimes with a technical essay or review.

  • Best for students who want to examine arguments, evidence, and structure directly.
  • Student-led mini-presentations increase as the group progresses.
  • Current offering: NeuroAI (minds, machines, and visual cortex).
