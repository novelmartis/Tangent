Pricing

Simple fee structure, discussed clearly on call.

Tangent is paid. Starting cohorts are subsidized, and details are finalized after fit and cohort placement.

Two tiers

Foundational

3–4 students 4 weeks

₹4,000 per student (India)

Advanced

4–5 students 4 weeks

₹5,000 per student (India)

For students outside India, a PPP-based conversion is used.

Subsidies
  • The first few cohorts are heavily subsidized.
  • Repeat students are also subsidized where possible.
  • Additional fee reductions can be discussed based on need.

Subsidy details are discussed on the call after fit and placement are clear.

How final fees are confirmed
  • Apply to a track.
  • Join a short fit call.
  • We confirm cohort placement and final fee details.
